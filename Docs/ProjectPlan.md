# Overview
Ping Ping Pong is a game about being forced to make strategic choices quickly and in rhythm.

Ultimately the gameplay loop will be growing a deck of strike cards that will build out towards particular synergies that will allow you to grow stronger and take on tougher competition over time. When entering a match you will shuffle your own cards in with negative cards from the opponent; 3 will appear at a time and you will need to use a shuffle or select one right as the ball bounces back off the table on your side.
